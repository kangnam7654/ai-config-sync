## Key Insights
- Korean job market prefers credit-based (건당) over subscription for career tools
- Saramin set the price anchor: 200-450원 per AI generation
- Human consultant benchmark: 20,000원/hour (40-80x AI pricing)
- IT/office worker job changers: ~600,000-900,000/year in Korea
- Freemium conversion for B2C career apps: expect 2-5% (Korean market may be lower due to price sensitivity)

## Structural Challenge: Low-Ticket Transaction Products
- LTV typically 7,000-15,000원 per job change cycle (1-2 purchases)
- Traditional CAC math breaks: LTV/CAC < 1:1 with any paid marketing
- Only viable path: organic growth (SEO, community, referral) where marginal CAC → 0
- Gross margins 80-90% but absolute profit per user too low to fund acquisition
