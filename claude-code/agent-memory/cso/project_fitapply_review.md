## fit-apply (맞춤지원) R1 Review — 2026-03-31

**Verdict: REVISE (Score 5.2/10)**

### Key Issues
1. **Korean incumbents already offer free AI features**: 사람인 AI 자소서 코칭 (Aug 2025, free), 잡코리아 AI 분석 (free), 잡메이커 (free), 코멘토, DeepResume, 하이잡 — CEO listed these as lacking but they've caught up
2. **Structural churn problem**: Resume builder SaaS = 15% monthly churn, ~85% annual churn. Users leave after finding job. LTV ~$101 (Rezi benchmark)
3. **SOM wildly overestimated**: CEO claims 60-170억 ARR at 1-3% penetration. Rezi (global, 2.6M users, Seoul-based, 11 people) = ~$5M ARR. Korean-only SaaS achieving 60억 ARR = unprecedented
4. **Product #5 with $0 revenue**: BuyBuddy (MVP done), FlirtIQ (approved), Dalgyeol (near launch), AgentBridge (OPPOSED) — adding another product before any revenue
5. **Employer AI detection rising**: 78% of companies check for AI content, 62% reject without personalization

### Benchmarks Discovered
- Rezi: $215K MRR, ~$2.5-5M ARR, 2.6M users, 11-person team, Seoul office, 55% margin
- Teal: 3.2M users, $20.7M total funding, Series A Jan 2025
- Jobscan: ~$7.6M revenue, bootstrapped, 60+ employees
- Korean 자소서 첨삭 WTP: 1-3만원 per session (not monthly subscription)
- Resume builder SaaS monthly churn: ~15%
- Free-to-paid conversion: 3-8%
- 사람인 AI 자소서 코칭: launched Aug 2025, free, includes AI detection bypass
- Z세대 91% use AI for 자소서, 20대 69.9% use AI 취업 services

**Why:** 5th product before any revenue, competing against free features from funded incumbents (사람인, 잡코리아), structural churn makes subscription model fragile

**How to apply:** If CEO insists, recommend: (1) validate WTP with 50 paid users before building, (2) per-document pricing not subscription, (3) defer until Dalgyeol launches and generates revenue
